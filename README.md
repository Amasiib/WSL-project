# 📖 WASL Platform | وَصـل

## 🎯 Project Overview
**WASL** is an advanced digital platform designed to revolutionize waste management operations in the Holy Sites. The platform adopts a data-driven approach that connects field operations with a central command center, ensuring the sustainability of the Holy Sites, reducing congestion, and accelerating response times according to the highest operational standards.

*"An integrated system that supports data-driven decision-making to enhance the quality of life in the Holy Sites, with a focus on User Experience (UX) to ensure ease of use for field observers."*

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### **Smart Sorting & IoT Integration**
* **Barcode Tagging:** Each waste container/bag is equipped with a unique barcode, enabling real-time tracking of waste origin and type, ensuring proper routing to recycling facilities.
* **IoT Sensor Integration:** Containers are equipped with smart sensors to monitor fill levels and waste material type, transforming traditional bins into intelligent data points that send real-time updates to the command center.
* **Precision Sorting:** Optimizing collection efficiency by dispatching trucks based on real-time "fill levels" and "material type" rather than static routes.

### **Command & Control**
* **Interactive Dashboard:** A comprehensive, real-time view of all operations and vital performance indicators.
* **Live GIS Operations Map:** Precise field tracking for assets and equipment.
* **Early Warning System:** Proactive alerts for potential risks (e.g., container overflow) to prevent crises before they occur.
* **Partner Performance Matrix:** An accurate assessment matrix measuring compliance and productivity for operational entities.
* **Waste Flow Analysis:** Visualizing the waste lifecycle from collection to recycling.

## 🏗 How It Works
1. **Field Tagging:** Staff scan the unique barcodes on collection units, instantly logging location, type, and time data into the system.
2. **Sensor Monitoring:** Smart sensors detect fill-level thresholds (e.g., >80%), triggering instant notifications to the platform to prevent congestion.
3. **Cloud Processing:** The platform cross-references "Barcode" data with "Sensor" readings to generate heatmaps identifying high-demand areas.
4. **Operational Response:** Alerts are displayed on the dashboard for decision-makers to dispatch field teams exactly where they are needed most.
